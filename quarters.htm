1440/1322/1320 2-Spliced Surprise Minor

by Graham A C John

23456   F   T   W   H            
53246       s       s   CC.LL. 
25346       s   s       CC.L.CC
32456   ss          s   C.LL.CCC.
34256               s   LLLLL.   
3 part

1440 Contains 720 Cambridge, 720 London; 23 com; atw.

For 1320 omit ssH and intervening leads on London in 2nd part. Contains each row once but not more than twice with no internal rounds. Contains 720 Cambridge, 600 London; 22 com; atw.

For 1322 call as 1320 and call bob Before in final course staying in London to come round at the backstroke snap. Contains 720 Cambridge, 602 London; 22 com; atw.

First rung as 1322 at West Wickham on 23 July 1975, conducted by Graham A C John.

1344 Kent and Oxford Treble Bob Major (Ilkeston variation)

by Graham A C John

23456   M   O3
42635       2
45362   1   4
23456   1   2

O3 = Oxford at 3 (i.e. call Oxford at the treble's 3-4 down dodge in the 3rd lead and back to Kent at the same point in the following lead of every course to prevent the tenors going in slow). For handbells.
