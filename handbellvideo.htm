This video was recorded during the change ringing on handbells session at the Ringing Roadshow held at Lincoln UK in July 2003. The clip shows an extract from one of the demonstration touches, namely a three-lead touch of Bristol Surprise Major rung by Michael Trimm 1-2, Andrew Tibbetts 3-4, Roger Bailey (hidden from view) 5-6, and Jonathan Hetherington 7-8.
